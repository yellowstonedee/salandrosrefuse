Self-Contained and Stationary Compactor Units

Are your current open top containers being picked once a week or more? A compactor may be a better solution to your refuse needs. Compactors on average will hold as much refuse as five open-top containers. Give us a call today to learn how much you could save by switching to a compactor.

Recycling

Salandro's Refuse, Inc. is committed to preserving the environment through our recycling efforts. Salandro's will accept your cardboard, scrap metal, and appliances free of charge*. On average Salandro's recycles over 300 Tons of cardboard per year that would otherwise go to local landfills. Do the right thing, don't landfill recyclable materials, bring them to us.

Salandro's accepts the following recyclable materials:

  • Old Corrugated Cardboard (OCC)
  • Scrap metal (steel, aluminum, stainless, etc...)
  • Appliances (freon* and non-freon)
    • *some fees may apply

About Us

In 1979 Salandro's Refuse, Inc. was established in Jeanette Pennsylvania with only a '68 Chevy pickup truck and friendly, reliable garbage service. From there the fleet quickly expanded and Salandro's bought their first rear load garbage truck in 1980, it was a '71 International. Salandro's continued to provide reliable residential curbside pickup and added Roll-off service in 1991. In 1995 residential pick-up was stopped to better focus on roll-off services. Today Salandro's operates a fleet of 6 roll-off trucks out of Crabtree, PA and provides services to Westmoreland and surrounding counties.
